Tasty Italian Starter Dish

Pancetta-Wrapped Halloumi Fries with Smoky Chilli Tomato Relish

Light & Tasty Starter

Ingredients

  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Crushed Garlic Clove
  • 1 Teaspoon Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon Chilli Flakes
  • 1 Tin (400g) Chopped Tomatoes
  • 3 Tablespoon Red Wine Vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoon Soft Dark brown Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Pack (250g) Halloumi
  • 1 Pack (95g) Sliced Pancetta
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley

Instructions

  1. For the relish: heat the oil in a saucepan. Add the chopped onion and cook gently for 10 minutes until softened. Add the crushed garlic clove, paprika, chilli flakes, then cook for another minute.
  2. Add the tin of chopped tomatoes, red wine vinegar, soft dark brown sugar and a pinch of salt. Simmer vir 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  3. Cut the halloumi into about 15 x 0.5cm sticks. take the pancetta and wrap a slice around each stick.
  4. Heat 1cm vegetable oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add the fries in batches and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Drain fries on a kitchen paper towel and keep warm on a oven tray in a low temperature oven. Sprinkle with chopped fresh flatleaf parsley and serve with the warm relish.
